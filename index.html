Toronto's Michelin-Rated Restaurants

Source: Canada's 100 Best (2022)


Toronto first received a Michelin Guide in 2022, and as of 2025 has 12 restaurants with one star and a single restaurant with two stars. No restaurants have received the prestigious honour of three stars, which is reserved for the greatest dining establishments in the world. The locations of Toronto's Michelin-rated restaurants can be found in this GeoJson file.

However, it should be noted that the best meals do not come cheap. The median cost of a tasting menu at a Michelin rated restaurant in Toronto was $295 per head in 2024. How much would you be willing to pay for a dinner at a Michelin restaurant?
